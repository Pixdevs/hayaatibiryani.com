About Us

Welcome to Hayaati Biryani, your new favorite destination for an unforgettable dining experience. Established in 2024, we are dedicated to bringing you the finest flavors from across the world, with a special focus on our signature biryani. Located in a beautifully designed space inspired by Arabic themes, our restaurant offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere for both casual dining and special occasions.
At Hayaati Biryani, we don’t just hire cooks – we train them to perfection. Every dish served is a result of meticulous care and expertise, ensuring that each bite meets our high standards of quality and taste. Our multi-cuisine menu offers a variety of dishes, but our biryani remains the star, crafted with authentic spices and ingredients that transport you straight to the heart of tradition.
Whether you're enjoying a meal in our clean, ambient dine-in space, taking food to go, or placing a bulk order for a special event, our commitment to exceptional customer service and excellence in food is unwavering.
Come, let us make your dining experience memorable at Hayaati Biryani – where passion for food meets tradition in every dish.
